The Syllabus

Why RSL Awards
matters.

RSL Awards is one of the UK's most respected music qualifications bodies — used in schools, conservatoires, and music centres across the country.

  • ✓Ofqual-regulated, internationally recognised qualifications
  • ✓Repertoire across classical, contemporary, rock, and pop
  • ✓Structured grades from Initial through to Grade 8
  • ✓Performance, theory, and technical skills developed together
  • ✓Grade certificates as tangible proof of achievement

A syllabus built for the real world.

Unlike school music lessons, our RSL pathway gives every child a clear progression route — from their very first note to a nationally recognised grade certificate. Every lesson is purposeful. Every term moves them forward.
